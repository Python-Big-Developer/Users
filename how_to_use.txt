How to use the Users Library:

Functions:
Adding a user: create_user(username)
Deleting a user: delte_user(username)
Editing a user: edit_user(username, new_username, new_id)

How to refer to lists and functions?
Users.name_of_function_or_list

What lists are in this library?
Logs - Stores the logs for the entire library.
USERNAMES - Stores the names of created users.
IDs - Stores the IDs of created users.
